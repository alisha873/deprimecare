Self Help Techniques

  1. Practice Mindfulness:

    Practicing mindfulness involves focusing on the present moment with an open and non-judgmental awareness. It encourages us to observe our thoughts, feelings, and sensations without getting caught up in them.

  2. Keep a Journal:

    Keeping a journal can be a powerful tool for self-help and personal growth. By dedicating time each day to reflect on your thoughts, feelings, and experiences, you create a safe space to explore your inner world.

  3. Exercise Regularly:

    Exercising regularly is a vital component of self-help and personal well-being. Engaging in physical activity not only improves your physical health but also significantly boosts your mental state.

  4. Reach Out to Friends or Family:

    Connecting with loved ones provides a support network that can uplift and inspire us during challenging times.

About Us

Déprimécare aims to provide mental health support to the student community all across India by providing a platform to reach out to well-trained and qualified mental health professionals as well as counsellors from their very own colleges while also maintaining an anonymous front to protect their identity.

This helps in creating a safe place where students can open up and seek help and support without the fear of being looked at in a negative way. The site connects students to mental health professionals and makes available their profiles so students can choose a professional they feel suits their needs and personal comfort level.

The other feature provided is the counsellor connect, which allows students to connect with the counsellors of their very own colleges, thus providing them with a sense of safety, knowing that they have the option to reach out to somebody around them. With the goal of creating a safe community which brings together students and counsellors, Déprimécare is here to render care to all those who need it with the hope of growing and improving with the community.
